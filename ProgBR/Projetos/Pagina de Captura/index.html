THE FIRST VALORANT COUPLE: From rivalry to love

Raze explodes out of Brazil with her big personality and big guns

Tayane Alves is an engineer coming from Salvador, Bahia, Brazil, with a strong enthusiasm for explosives and paint - usually combined. After force Kingdom Corporation's to abandon her region, she entered to the VALORANT Protocol as its twelfth agent, "Raze", Alves now sows her chaos against bigger threats to Earth. However, as the paint fades from Salvador's walls, Raze cannot afford to leave her home completely behind.

The genius of Germany uses her self-made inventions to assist her in battle

Klara Böhringer is an inventor from Germany and a nominee for the Distinguished Inventor award, who had become the lead for Kingdom's R&D department at the age of 18. Incredibly successful, she had many breakthroughs during her time at Kingdom with several of her creations and innovations. Böhringer was the fourth agent to join the VALORANT Protocol as "Killjoy".
